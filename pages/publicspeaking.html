Round 2 - On the Spot Debate

Process

  • Debate Of 2 or 4 students(According to Participation).

  • For/Against Will Be Decided by Chits.

  • Topic Will Be Given On The Spot.

  • 5 minutes To Prepare Before Debate.

Judging Criteria(Prizes Based On Scores)

  • Content and Relevance(20 points).

  • Presentation Of Argument(10 points).

  • Body Language(20 points).

  • Verbal Skills(20 points).

  • Vocabulary(10 points).

  • Confidence(10 points).

  • Fluency(10 points).
