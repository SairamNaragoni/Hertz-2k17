Rules

  • Total Five Rounds.

  • Scores Will Be Calculated From the Second Round.

  • Scores of Previous Round Will Be Carried Over Till End.

  • Teams Will Be Eliminated in Each Round.

  • Scores and Other Related Details Would be Given at the Beginning of Each Round.

Round-1

Basic Quiz

  • Pen and Paper Based.

  • 2 Members in Each Team.

  • Total 30 Questions in Paper.

  • 30 Minutes.

  • 1 Mark for Correct Answer and No -ve for Wrong Answers.

Round-2

Visual Round

  • 3 Turns.

  • 1 Question For Each Team in Each Turn.

  • 45sec For Each Question and 10sec For Passed Question.

  • 20 Marks For Each Correct Answer and 10 Marks For Passed Answer.

  • 1 Pass(To the Next Team Only) and No -ve Marking.

Round-3

Raising the Bar

  • 1 Turn.

  • 3 Questions For Each Team.

  • 30sec For 1st Question,45sec for 2nd Quetion,1 minute for 3rd Question .

  • 3rd Question is Pick-Up a Chit Based.

  • 1st Question is of 10marks and if Wrong -5 Marks.

  • 2st Question is of 20marks and if Wrong -10 Marks.

  • 3rd Question is of 30marks and if Wrong -15 Marks.

Round-4

Rapid Fire

  • 1 Turn.

  • Maximum 10 Questions For Each term and 90sec Time Limit.

  • 30sec For 1st Question,45sec for 2nd Question,1 minute for 3rd Question .

  • 20 marks For Each Correct Answer, -10 For Each Wrong and Zero For Not Answering.

  • No Passing.

Round-5

Bidding Round

  • Bidding Time Should Be in Multiples of 5.

  • Max Bid Time = 50 sec.

  • Question Will Be Given to the Lowest Time Bidder .

  • Correct Answer Within Bidtime = 60-{Biddind Sec}.

  • Wrong Answer = -{Bid Sec}/2.

  • No Passing.
