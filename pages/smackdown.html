Objective

  • The only objective in this game is to knock off the opponent bot by brute force or clever tactics.

  • Last man standing wins the sparring.

Arena Specifications

  • Flat Elevated Cube 55 Ft.

  • Surface may vary in three stages of the tournament like sand, oil, marbles etc.

  • Four Bots will fight in one time sparring.

Robot Specifications

  • Regular farm track bots would be best in this tournament.

  • The bot should fit into a 35x35x35 cm cube.

  • However the participant is allowed to perform any modifications on the bot unless it causes permenant damage to the opponent bot.

Scoring

  • Points will be awarded for every successful knock off.

  • Points will be given to the last bot standing in each round.

  • In case of any dispute the decision of event head will be final.

Rules

  • Four bots fight to survive.

  • Teams of two.

  • Three stages in tournaments.

  • Three sparrings per match.
