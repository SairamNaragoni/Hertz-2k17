About

HERTZ is a maiden Electronics Mega-Fest organized by IETE Student's Forum, National Institute Of Technology Surat. It is biggest of it's kind in Gujarat. Hertz is a three day festival consisting of informal, technical and other exciting events like Coding and Public Speaking. It is open to all, so sit back, enjoy and resonate with Hertz.

Heinrich Rudolf Hertz (22 February 1857 – 1 January 1894) was a German physicist who first conclusively proved the existence of electromagnetic waves theorized by James Clerk Maxwell's electromagnetic theory of light. The unit of frequency – cycle per second – was named the "hertz" in his honor.
